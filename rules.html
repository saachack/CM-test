Rules

What is Cyber Maze?

Cyber Maze is a cyber security competition (jeopardy CTF) targeted to UG college students. Challenges covers a broad range of topics from cryptography, forensics, reverse engineering, web penetration and more.


How do I participate in the competition?

Register for this event by clicking register tab and solve the 5 tiny challenges on the registration page. We will send you the confirmation email along with payment link if you are selected. To confirm your seat for the event kindly make a payment of 400 INR. On-spot payment is also available.

When does the CTF start and end?

The CTF starts on Aug 2 6:00 pm and ends on the next day 6:00 at Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology, Chennai. After the CTF ends, walk through will be given for each challenge. Rooms for resting wil be provided for participants with all facilities needed. Transport facilities will be provided for the partcipants, with common pickup points.

How are the winners determined?

The Top 3 individuals that accumulates the most points within the allotted time will be the winners. If more than one individual solves all of the problems or reaches the same top score total, then individual who has solved the challenge in less time will be considered.


What are things required?

Participants are asked to bring your own laptops along with charging cables and a pendrive for sharing files/resouces.


What are the prizes?

We have prizes upto 50,000 INR and more exciting prizes are available Hack, Learn and Earn them. We also exciting goodies like t-shirts and so on.
