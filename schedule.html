Schedule

While organizing Cyber_Maze we chose quality over quantity.
We are convinced that a few exciting, hand-picked surprises would make a great CTF experience while not distracting you from actually working on the challenges.

The official start of Cyber_Maze is at 06:00 pm, before that the partcipants are asked to confirm their admission and collect their goodies... If you happen to be early however, we got you covered, take a look at our amazing campus, network around and enjoy some fresh filter coffee amidst the greenery.

A special guest will be present to kick the event off, along with an interaction session with our Speaker.

Get your Night Owl mode ON, hackers work at night... get ready for 12 hours of intense hacking.

It’s time for the only thing that really matters : food. Take your time to enjoy a fresh and delicious meal servered at our canteen. Quick, grab a bite and get back to hacking or stay around and network. It’s your choice.

Hacking continues!

Intense Hacking for prolonged periods can drain you out. This part is a Surprise tbh.

Ya you read that right.. We have an DJ.

The event ends the next morning at 06:00 am.

The live walkthrough session will be given for each challenge.

Delicious Breakfast would be provided for the partcipants.

Prizes worth 50,000 would be given to the winners.
